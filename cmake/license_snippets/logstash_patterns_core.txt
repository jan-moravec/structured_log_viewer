logstash-patterns-core (https://github.com/logstash-plugins/logstash-patterns-core)
is not linked into this binary, but several PCRE2 regex templates shipped with
the Structured Log Viewer's regex parser are adapted from its grok patterns.
Per Apache-2.0, attribution and a copy of the license are preserved here.

The patterns adapted from logstash-patterns-core are in
library/src/regex_templates.cpp (search for "grok"):
  - Apache/nginx Common Log Format -- adapted from `COMMONAPACHELOG`
  - Apache/nginx Combined Log Format -- adapted from `COMBINEDAPACHELOG`
